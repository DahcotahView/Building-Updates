A friendly space for neighbors — give away furniture, share extra food, post a lost-and-found, or give the community a helpful heads-up. Kindness required, negativity not accepted.

How this board works: submitted posts are reviewed by property management before appearing here — usually within a business day or two. Posts must be kind, helpful, and neighborly. Anything negative, hateful, or targeting a neighbor or staff member will not be published. Have a concern or complaint? We genuinely want to hear it — bring it to us directly through the Contact page where we can actually help, rather than the community board where we can't.

Submit a Post

Posts are not anonymous to management — we ask for your name, unit, and contact so we can reach you and keep this board safe and accountable. Your personal details are never published unless you choose to display contact info on your post.

📷 Have a photo of your item? Mention it in your post, or email it to DVABurnsville@gmail.com with your post title — we can include it when your post is published.

Your post will be reviewed by management before it appears on this page.
