Wondering where your rent is supposed to go? You're in the right place — here's everything about the rent drop box, so your payment lands safely every month.

Where Is the Rent Drop Box?

The rent drop boxes are located between the front entry doors for both 1601 and 1605. They are the white boxes seen right in the entrance on the wall next to the call box system. It's a secure, locked drop box — once your payment goes in, only office staff can retrieve it.

The drop box is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — so even if the office is closed, your rent never has to be late.

How to Submit Your Rent

  • Pay by check or money order only. Never place cash in the drop box.
  • Write your full name, building, and unit number on the check or money order memo line so your payment is credited to the right apartment.
Please never deposit cash. Cash can't be traced to your unit if anything goes wrong, and we can't accept responsibility for cash payments left in the box.

Questions about your balance, a receipt, or payment options? Reach out through the Contact page or call the office at 952-890-7120.
