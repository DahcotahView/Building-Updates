🎉 Something to celebrate? We love that for you! Our community party room is here for birthdays, graduations, showers, game days, and every festive occasion in between.

How It Works

Before you submit your request, here's the important part: there is a $200 refundable deposit due before receiving the party room access keys. Here's the full process from request to celebration:

  • Request your date using the form below.
  • Management reviews your request and confirms the date is available.
  • Stop by the office at a time we set up together to sign our Party Room Policy Agreement — our room usage rules that keep the space respectful and ready for the next celebration.
  • Before your party date, bring a check written out for $200 as the room deposit. Checks only — NOT cash.
  • Get your keys! We'll arrange to provide the room keys the day of or the day before your requested party day — the Friday before, if your party falls on a weekend.
  • Celebrate, then get your deposit back. Upon respectful usage of the room — no damage and no violations of the signed agreement — your deposit check is returned to you promptly.
